Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Take a bowl, add gulab jamun powder in it.
  2. Add sufficent milk to it, mix the powder and make round sized ball with the mixture.
  3. Hot the oil, keep the round sized balls in it.
  4. Take another bowl, add sugar and water heat it.
  5. After the sugar mixture cool downs, keep the round sized balls in it. Gulab Jamun is ready.
